How can I help?

There are many ways you can help us achieve our vision of connecting people in Bern, here are a few of them, thanks so much for reading!

📢 Spread the word!

The best way to help us is to spread the word! Write a message directly to your friends and acquaintances in Bern, follow our Instagram, share our flyers on Instagram or other social media, or bring new people to our place or events! Every new person who hears about us, helps us to grow the community and keep it alive!

🎯

Help With Event Planning

We have a space to create events that bring people together and foster genuine connections, and we need your help making that happen! Visit our GitHub event issues page to write comments, give tips, contribute ideas, or add a completely new event idea!

🤝

Help At Events

You can also help at the events themselves: come early and help with organizing, stay a bit later to clean up, help welcoming new people, share ideas on how to improve things. Just expressing your interest in helping already goes a long way! It helps enormously when we have people around who have spent some time trying to understand the mission and the vision of the project.

💝 Financial Support

The rent is expensive - that's the main reason we need the money. We have a central place in Bern with a great landlord and no problems with the neighbors, and we'd love to keep it! If you have ideas on how to help the project financially, we'd love to hear them.

💡 Got creative ideas?

We're always looking for new, creative ways to fund the project! Do you have ideas, or can you or anyone you know help us with the existing ideas?

For example:

  • 🤝 Sponsorship opportunities - Know any companies that support social projects?
  • 📝 Grants & Funding Programs - Do you know of any programs? We're already aware of Migros Kultur. Or perhaps you have experience with grant applications or know someone who could help us?
  • 💭 Share your ideas - Do you know of any other ideas of how we could fund ourselves?
💬 Contact us with your ideas

Want to contribute? Here's how 💛

Donations help us do more — buy snacks, decorations, plants, maybe a cleaner, and reach more people online. None of this is critical, but it genuinely makes the space nicer and helps us grow. If you have a little to spare, we'd really appreciate it. 💙
