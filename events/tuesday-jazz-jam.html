Tuesday Jazz Jam

Weekly jam session at 5ème ETAGE

The Tuesday Jam is Bern's meeting point for the active jazz scene. Every Tuesday, local and international musicians gather for a public jam session.

🤝 Connect Bern Meeting Point

We meet together at Zytglogge at 7:30 PM and head to the jam together. So no one has to go alone!

What to Expect

⏰ Schedule

  • 8:00 PM - Doors open, risotto is served
  • 9:00 PM - Live jam band starts (about 1.5 hours)
  • After - Open jam session for everyone
  • 12:30 AM - Session ends

🍽️ Food & Drinks

Starting at 8 PM, there's delicious risotto (vegetarian) for CHF 5, prepared by an Italian chef. Optional Wienerli for CHF 5 extra, salad for CHF 5.

🎵 The Music

A professional jam band (guitar/piano, bass, drums, sometimes sax) plays from 9 PM. After that, the session is open to all musicians – bring your instrument or just come to listen!

✨ Atmosphere

Small, cozy, right under the roof – an intimate spot with great vibes. Space is limited, so it's recommended to arrive early.

📍 Location & Getting There

5ème ETAGE, Mühlenplatz 11, Matte Bern (accessible by elevator)

Easily accessible by bus no. 30, stops directly at Mühlenplatz and goes to the train station.

🗺️ Open Google Maps

💰 Costs

💵 CHF 5 entry
🍚 CHF 5 risotto (optional)

The money goes directly to the Tuesday Jam association, not to Connect Bern. We only organize going together so no one has to go alone.
