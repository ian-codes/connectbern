Transportation

WARNING: Beware of the price changes on the SBB app - the price is actually double!

Long-distance Traveling

  • If you want to hitchhike:

    • https://hitchspots.me
    • https://hitchwiki.org/en/Bern
  • You can get a ride on https://blablacar.com for around 10 franks. If you are really desperate you can also try blablacar daily and ask the driver if you can come for only one trip.
  • Traveling via Flixbus is also possible inside of Switzerland. It's illegal for Flixbus to offer rides inside of Switzerland (Kabotageverbots) so they don't do it but it you buy a ticket to Freiburg in Germany when you're going to Basel from Bern you can just get off in Basel and it's still much cheaper in some cases than paying for the train.   • If you're very lucky you can maybe find a ride for free in these groups To and from Bern: WhatsApp: https://tiny.cc/mitfahrenbernwhatsapp Telegram: https://tiny.cc/mitfahrenbern Other cities, if you want to also try your luck in other similiar groups besides the Bernese one you can look for the "mitfahren" or "mitfahrgelegenheit" here: https://t.me/unkomarkt_sammlung (there is also 1 for all of Switzerland)
  • You can search for a cheap (between Fr. 40 - 52) daily ticket for all of Switzerland on this website https://www.tageskarte-gemeinde.ch/ sometimes they have an option to send it by post (for example to my place) if you think about it early enough, otherwise you need to go to the place you are buying the ticket from which might be far and also cost money if you don't want to hitchhike or bike there....
  • If you are under 25 years old, you can travel around switzerland for one year for 99 francs, always after 7pm. Some gondola lifts and boats are included. you can buy it here: https://www.sbb.ch/de/kampagne/night.html Here is the map showing where in Switzerland you can travel for free with this ticket: https://www.sbb.ch/de/abos-billette/abonnemente

Parking

Parking spots with blue line are only free on sunday. But also not every blue parking zone. Always read the signs carefully, use google lens to translate if needed. There is a ticket to buy to park the car on the blue lines:

  • 19 francs for 24 hours
  • 9 francs for 4 hours
